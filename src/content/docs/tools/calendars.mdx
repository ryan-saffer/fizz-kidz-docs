To add a calendar, click the relevant calendar link below while signed in to your Fizz Kidz Google account.

<Aside type="caution" title="Multiple Google accounts can cause issues">
  If you are signed in to multiple Google accounts, the calendar might attach to
  the wrong account or fail to load. Sign out of extra accounts first.
</Aside>
