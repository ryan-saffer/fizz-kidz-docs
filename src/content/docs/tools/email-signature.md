Use the link below to create a beautiful Fizz Kidz email signature.

- [Open the Fizz Kidz Signature Creator](https://sites.google.com/fizzkidz.com.au/fizz-kidz-signature-creator?usp=sharing)

## How to use it

1. Enter your details in the signature creator.
2. Click **Select signature**.
3. Copy the signature with `Cmd + C` on Mac or `Ctrl + C` on Windows.
4. In Gmail, go to **Settings** > **See all settings**.
5. Scroll down to **Signature** and paste it in.
